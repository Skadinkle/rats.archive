Basic Info:

The Corealis (more commenly known as a Corey) is a humanoid-like entity commonly found among regions in North and South America, primarily in Ohio. It is currently unclear of what their motives are, but it is known that they seek computer files.


Characteristics / Description:

The Corey commonly appears as a regular human entity with peachy skin, dark brown hair, brown eyes, and a unibrow. They are dressed in a variety of different clothing, from sweaters, coats, t-shirts, etc. They are ofter compared to the protagonist of the childrens animated tv show, Steven Universe (see Figure. 1).
